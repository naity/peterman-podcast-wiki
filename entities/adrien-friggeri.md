# Adrien Friggeri

Principal engineer (IC8) at [Meta](../entities/meta.md), original tech lead of Bento (Meta's company-wide Jupyter notebook platform), who joined Facebook straight from a PhD in social network analysis and climbed to IC8 with detours through a failed startup and a year at Clubhouse ([episode](../sources/new-grad-to-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).

## Career arc

Friggeri joined Meta from his PhD; his first manager corrected his data-scientist pretension on day one ("Software engineers have power at this company... don't pigeonhole yourself"). His first project showed false rumors spread faster on Facebook than true ones and that debunking barely helps — work that fed into down-weighting reshares. His IC5 promo came from building [Instagram](../entities/instagram.md)'s first A/B testing system as a naive IC4 during the AWS-to-Facebook migration, hacking exposure logging through an allow-listed API after being told "don't bug us." Bento began as personal helper scripts to escape data-pipeline boilerplate; he negotiated 2–3 months of explicit perf risk with his manager and skip, grew it into the platform used by ~60% of Meta's data practitioners, and earned IC7/TLM2. He left in 2018 to found a [startup](../concepts/startups-and-founding.md), learned he wasn't an entrepreneur ("I left the company to do a startup, and I ended up taking a sabbatical instead"), and boomeranged back twice — both times through trusted former managers. His IC8 came as experiences tech lead across an unreleased smart-glasses hardware line ([episode](../sources/new-grad-to-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).

## Key views & advice

- Senior [promotions](../concepts/promotions.md) are consensus built ahead of time: for IC8 he co-wrote the packet with a manager who'd never done one, socialized with multiple VPs a cycle early, and asked a critical D2 for monthly check-ins six months ahead — "you don't have a debate in the room." But "getting promoted is not just a social game... you need the underlying work too" ([calibrations-and-ratings](../concepts/calibrations-and-ratings.md), [episode](../sources/new-grad-to-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
- Internal-tool adoption strategy: don't fight existing workflows — capture newcomers at bootcamp in a fast-growing company and support the legacy systems to build trust before migrating users ([influence-and-leadership](../concepts/influence-and-leadership.md), [developer-tools](../concepts/developer-tools.md), [episode](../sources/new-grad-to-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
- Taking perf risk on exploratory work: make it an explicitly agreed expectation with manager and skip, over-communicate, be ready to hear no — the strategy is unchanged even though the consequences of underperforming are harsher today ([episode](../sources/new-grad-to-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
- Scope is taken off someone's plate through over-communication, not stolen — he carved out the smart-glasses "TL for experiences" role by visibly taking coordination load off the existing uber TL with his blessing; the label came post hoc after a year of doing the work ([episode](../sources/new-grad-to-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
- Every career change came through relationships — he has never applied for a job through a website ([mentorship-and-sponsorship](../concepts/mentorship-and-sponsorship.md), [non-linear-careers](../concepts/non-linear-careers.md), [episode](../sources/new-grad-to-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
- His biggest self-described mistake: never officially joining early Instagram while embedded there — peers from that ~30-person team reached IC7–IC10 far faster ([regrets-and-advice](../concepts/regrets-and-advice.md), [episode](../sources/new-grad-to-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
- General [advice](../concepts/regrets-and-advice.md): be well-rounded ("specialization is for insects" — Heinlein), foster success around you without expecting returns ("the best way to succeed is to help others succeed"), take more shots to increase your luck surface area, and be patient — pushing for IC7 before he was ready cost him cycles ([episode](../sources/new-grad-to-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
