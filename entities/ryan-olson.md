# Ryan Olson

Principal engineer (IC8) at [Instagram](../entities/instagram.md) who led iOS for Instagram Stories, IGTV, and IG Labs; now co-founder of the friends-only social app Retro ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-eng-ic8-on-building.md)).

## Career arc

Olson failed his 2011 Facebook internship interview so badly the interviewer cut it short and told him "you're just not gonna be able to make it at Facebook." He interned instead at ~50-person Flipboard alongside future billionaires Dylan Field (Figma) and Devin Finzer (OpenSea). His open-source iOS debugging tool FLEX got him into Instagram — an engineer saw it and advocated for him after a mixed loop. From mis-leveled IC4 he climbed to IC8 at [Meta](../entities/meta.md): IC5 for the Whiteout redesign, IC6 for Stories (built in ~2-3 months by a core of two iOS engineers, beating [Snapchat](../entities/snapchat.md) at everyday sharing via Instagram's friend-graph advantage), IC7 for IGTV with Kevin Systrom (a failure — long-form vertical inventory never appeared), and IC8 for founding IG Labs, the tiny innovation group that later seeded Threads. He left to co-found Retro with Stories PM Nathan Sharp: mutual-friends-only, 250-friend cap, no ads, subscription-funded, #1 on Taiwan's App Store ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-eng-ic8-on-building.md)).

## Key views & advice

- "If you want to go fast, go small": Stories succeeded because he *cut* the team down; each marginal engineer adds hidden coordination and codebase cost — he was waiting 4+ hours a day on builds before leaving, and thinks Instagram would likely run better with half the people ([small-teams](../concepts/small-teams.md), [episode](../sources/instagram-principal-eng-ic8-on-building.md)).
- [Promotions](../concepts/promotions.md) are an incentive system — "the carrot that's being dangled in front of you" — and delays are often pacing, not performance. His hack: deliberately ask for an impossible double promo so the refusal converts into back-to-back promotions ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-eng-ic8-on-building.md)).
- A/B testing is invaluable for conversion mechanics, but product direction needs strong opinions — pure experimentation traps you in 1% incrementalism and "you're never going to get that 50% jump" ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-eng-ic8-on-building.md)).
- He rejected Facebook's IC/[management](../concepts/management.md) separation as a rare Tech Lead Director (preferring Tesla's flexible model, citing Taleb's skin in the game), and paid for it in ratings: "you're exceeding as an IC but you're meeting as a manager, so you're just meeting" ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-eng-ic8-on-building.md)).
- Ads plus algorithmic feeds create "a little bit evil" misaligned incentives — the founding thesis of Retro's subscription model ([startups-and-founding](../concepts/startups-and-founding.md), [episode](../sources/instagram-principal-eng-ic8-on-building.md)).
- His case against leetcode-style [interviews](../concepts/hiring-and-interviews.md) is his own life; practically, he used prescribed beta blockers to break the physical nerves feedback loop ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-eng-ic8-on-building.md)).
- [Advice](../concepts/regrets-and-advice.md) to new grads: "learn the tools of your time" — he stood out because iOS was new; today's equivalent is AI tooling, where new grads can out-skill seniors ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-eng-ic8-on-building.md)).
