# Philip Su

Distinguished Engineer (IC9) at Meta, later at OpenAI, and ex-Microsoft; founded and scaled Facebook's London office and later voluntarily requested a demotion from IC9 to IC7 ([episode](../sources/openai-and-meta-distinguished-engineer.md)).

## Career arc

Started at [Microsoft](microsoft.md), then moved to [Meta](meta.md), where he reached IC9 by founding and scaling Facebook's London office from around a dozen engineers to 500+ — the blast radius that made IC9-level impact possible. Across his career he switched between IC and engineering manager six times, choosing roles by business need and personal enjoyment. He eventually requested a demotion from IC9 to IC7 to do more technical work, relieving IC9 expectations and preserving role-switching flexibility, and later worked at [OpenAI](openai.md) ([episode](../sources/openai-and-meta-distinguished-engineer.md)).

## Key views & advice

- IC9 expectations are defined by the blast radius you have and what you can guarantee the success of ([episode](../sources/openai-and-meta-distinguished-engineer.md)).
- The highest-level [promotions](../concepts/promotions.md) are not a checklist of quantitative points; they run on qualitative feedback, requiring the judgment of someone at a higher level to explain what makes the work special ([episode](../sources/openai-and-meta-distinguished-engineer.md)).
- For generalists there is a lot of commonality between [management](../concepts/management.md) and tech leadership, which enables switching; but generalists are much more effective at smaller companies — at larger companies specialists have more potential because they can go as deep as they want ([episode](../sources/openai-and-meta-distinguished-engineer.md)).
- [Working with legends](../concepts/working-with-legends.md): what distinguished Mark Zuckerberg was his constant devotion to growth; John Carmack impressed him with prolific engineering ability — able to drop into a codebase he knew nothing about and give excellent feedback ([episode](../sources/openai-and-meta-distinguished-engineer.md)).
- On [writing](../concepts/teaching-and-communication.md): to write well, read examples of good writing and take inspiration from them ([episode](../sources/openai-and-meta-distinguished-engineer.md)).
- His voluntary demotion and six IC/EM switches are the podcast's clearest example of a [non-linear career](../concepts/non-linear-careers.md) at the highest IC levels ([episode](../sources/openai-and-meta-distinguished-engineer.md)).
