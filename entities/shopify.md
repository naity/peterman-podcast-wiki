# Shopify

E-commerce platform company that appears in the podcast as the current home of [Ilya Grigorik](ilya-grigorik.md), Distinguished Engineer (L10) — a VP-level role he reached after founding PostRank and a Director stint at [Google](google.md) ([Shopify Distinguished Eng](../sources/distinguished-engineer-at-shopify.md)).

The episode's Shopify-specific contribution is what Distinguished-level engineering means there: L10 is VP-equivalent and defined by demonstrated dynamic range — technically from bare metal up to business requirements, and in execution mode from fog-of-war startup iteration to slow, careful platform/API design with years-long second-order consequences ([systems-design](../concepts/systems-design.md)) ([episode](../sources/distinguished-engineer-at-shopify.md)). Grigorik's operating philosophy at that level: anyone who asks for a checklist job description isn't ready — you're "parachuted into foreign terrain" and expected within about seven days to map the land and find where your skillset applies — and his measure of success is making himself unnecessary, the "Homer Simpson disappearing into the bushes" test ([episode](../sources/distinguished-engineer-at-shopify.md)). His signature career advice, "don't be the best, be the only," frames the portfolio of 80–90% competencies such a role demands ([career-growth](../concepts/career-growth.md)) ([episode](../sources/distinguished-engineer-at-shopify.md)).
