# Jake Bolam

Principal Engineer (IC8) at [Instagram](instagram.md), known for an unusually permissive code-review philosophy and for working ~40 hours a week at Principal level ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).

## Career arc

Joined [Meta](meta.md) as a Staff Engineer (IC6) and initially struggled on a team that didn't value his frontend skillset; his career took off after switching to an [Instagram](instagram.md) team that did. His [promotion](../concepts/promotions.md) to IC8 came from a migration that started multi-team and grew until it involved moving all of Instagram's backend to an entirely new backend, affecting hundreds of teams — the blast radius plus project success despite the risk earned the promotion ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).

## Key views & advice

- On diff review he biases toward "how can I accept this diff" rather than "how can I reject this diff": if a diff isn't risky or touching a critical path, he prioritizes speed and gives a rudimentary review, pushing the envelope on project speed unless told he's moving too fast ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
- "Go where you're valued" is his advice to his younger self — [career growth](../concepts/career-growth.md) stalled on a team that didn't value his skillset and accelerated on one that did ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
- Works ~40 hours a week even at IC8 via systems built over his career: blocking out focus time and minimizing meetings far more aggressively than the average engineer ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
- His note-taking system is a mass of deliberately unorganized plain text files in VS Code, navigated by remembered keywords and cross-linked like a personal Wikipedia ([episode](../sources/instagram-principal-engineer-ic8.md)).
